About Akij Takaful

Akij Takaful Life Insurance PLC was established in 2021 in order to meet the increasing demand for life insurance in Bangladesh. At Akij Takaful Life Insurance Plc, our policyholders are our number one priority. We offer a broad range of life insurance policies so that our policyholders can have healthier, happier and longer lives. Our products are designed keeping in mind the needs of people from all stages of life, professions and income groups.
